Vowzy Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026

Overview

Vowzy is designed to help you track habits, guided journeys, reminders, and wellbeing-related progress. We aim to collect only what is needed for the app to work.

Data Stored in the App

Vowzy may store habit names, guided journey progress, reminder settings, completion history, selected themes, and premium journey purchase status.

iCloud

If iCloud is available, Vowzy may use Apple’s iCloud and CloudKit services to sync and back up your app data across your devices. Apple’s own privacy terms apply to iCloud storage.

Apple Health

Vowzy can request permission to read step count data from Apple Health for optional step-proof habits. Vowzy does not write workout or health samples.

Purchases

Premium journey purchases are handled by Apple StoreKit. Vowzy uses purchase status to unlock purchased journeys.

Support Emails

If you contact support, we will use your email and message only to respond to your request.

Contact

For privacy questions, email slinkywizardpublications@gmail.com.
